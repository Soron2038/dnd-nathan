## Initiative Proficiency

Add Proficiency Bonus when rolling Initiative.

## Initiative Swap

Immediately after rolling Initiative, Nathan can swap his Initiative with one willing ally in the same combat. The swap is unavailable if Nathan or that ally has the Incapacitated condition.

## Nathan implementation

- Source: Custom Background, confirmed
- current Initiative modifier: Dexterity +3 plus Proficiency Bonus +2 = +5
- Initiative Swap becomes available whenever a qualifying willing ally participates
