# Nathan's Spells — provided 2024 rules text

Status: vom Spieler am 2026-07-21 bereitgestellt; verbindliche Quelle.

Nathan's Spell Save DC is 13 and Spellcasting Ability modifier is +3.

## Prestidigitation

- Cantrip; Transmutation
- 1 Action; 10 feet; V, S; up to 1 hour
- choose Sensory Effect, Fire Play, Clean or Soil, Minor Sensation, Magic Mark, or Minor Creation
- up to three non-instantaneous effects can coexist
- Minor Creation lasts only until the end of Nathan's next turn, fits in his hand, has no monetary value and deals no damage

## Minor Illusion

- Cantrip; Illusion
- 1 Action; 30 feet; S, M; 1 minute
- creates either a sound or an image of an object no larger than a 5-foot Cube
- only one casting can remain active
- Study action and successful Intelligence (Investigation) against DC 13 reveals it
- physical interaction reveals an image; an image creates no sound, light, smell, or other sensory effect

## Dissonant Whispers

- Level 1; Enchantment
- 1 Action; 60 feet; V; instantaneous
- one visible creature makes Wisdom save against DC 13
- failure: 3d6 Psychic damage and immediate Reaction, if available, to move as far away as possible by safest route
- success: half damage only
- +1d6 damage per slot level above 1

## Detect Magic

- Level 1; Divination; Concentration; Ritual
- 1 Action; Self, 30-foot area; V, S; up to 10 minutes
- senses magical effects; a Magic action reveals a faint aura on a visible bearer and the school if created by a Spell
- blocked by 1 foot of stone/dirt/wood, 1 inch of metal, or a thin sheet of lead

## Command

- Level 1; Enchantment
- 1 Action; 60 feet; V; instantaneous
- one visible creature makes Wisdom save against DC 13
- on failure, follows one selected printed command on its next turn: Approach, Drop, Flee, Grovel, or Halt
- one additional target per slot level above 1

## Cure Wounds

- Level 1; Abjuration
- 1 Action; Touch; V, S; instantaneous
- restores 2d8 + 3 HP
- +2d8 per slot level above 1; with a Level-2 slot restores 4d8 + 3 HP

## Suggestion

- Level 2; Enchantment; Concentration
- 1 Action; 30 feet; V, M; up to 8 hours
- no more than 25 words to one visible creature that can hear and understand Nathan
- suggestion must sound achievable and not obviously damage target or allies
- Wisdom save against DC 13; failure causes Charmed while target pursues suggestion
- ends on completion, duration expiry, Concentration loss, or damage from Nathan or his allies

## Silence

- Level 2; Illusion; Concentration; Ritual
- 1 Action; 120 feet; V, S; 20-foot-radius Sphere; up to 10 minutes
- no sound is created within or passes through the Sphere
- creatures and objects entirely inside have Immunity to Thunder damage
- creatures entirely inside are Deafened
- Spells with Verbal components cannot be cast there

## Concentration conflicts

Only one can be maintained at a time:

- Detect Magic
- Suggestion
- Silence
