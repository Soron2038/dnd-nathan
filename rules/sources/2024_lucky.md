Type: Origin Feat

## Luck Points

Luck Points equal Proficiency Bonus. All expended points return after a Long Rest.

At Character Level 4, Nathan has 2 Luck Points.

## Advantage

When Nathan rolls a d20 for a D20 Test, he can spend 1 Luck Point to give himself Advantage on the roll.

## Disadvantage

When a creature rolls a d20 for an attack roll against Nathan, he can spend 1 Luck Point to impose Disadvantage on that roll.

## Resource tracking

`Lucky` and Human `Resourceful` are independent:

- 2 Luck Points per Long Rest at Level 4
- Heroic Inspiration gained whenever Nathan finishes a Long Rest
