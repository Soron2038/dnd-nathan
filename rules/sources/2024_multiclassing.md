# Multiclassing — provided 2024 rules text

Status: vom Spieler am 2026-07-21 vollständig bereitgestellt; verbindliche Quelle für diese Kampagne.

## Prerequisites

To enter a new class, a Character needs at least 13 in the Primary Ability of the new class and all current classes.

Nathan has Dexterity 16 for Rogue and Charisma 16 for Bard, satisfying both prerequisites.

## Hit Points and Hit Dice

- Level-1 Hit Points apply only to the class taken at total Character Level 1: Rogue.
- Bard levels grant Hit Points as described for levels after 1.
- Combine Hit Dice from all classes; Nathan has four d8 Hit Dice at Rogue 1 / Bard 3.
- Fixed values are used. With Constitution modifier +1, Nathan has 9 HP from Rogue Level 1 and 6 HP from each of three Bard levels, for 27 HP total.

## Proficiency Bonus

Based on total Character Level. Nathan is Level 4 and has Proficiency Bonus +2.

## Proficiencies

Only the Multiclass Bard proficiencies listed in the Bard class are gained: one Skill, one Musical Instrument, and Light Armor training.

## Spellcasting

The Multiclass Spellcaster table is used only after a Character has Spellcasting from more than one class. Nathan has Spellcasting only from Bard and therefore follows the Bard table directly.

At Bard 3:

- 4 Level-1 Spell Slots
- 2 Level-2 Spell Slots
- 2 Cantrips
- 6 prepared Bard Spells

Cantrip scaling uses total Character Level unless the Spell states otherwise.
