# Objects — official 2024 Basic Rules

Source: https://www.dndbeyond.com/sources/dnd/br-2024/rules-glossary

Status: official 2024 rule reference; verified 2026-07-21.

## Breaking Objects

- A fragile nonmagical object can sometimes be broken automatically with an Attack or Utilize action.
- More resilient objects use material-based Armor Class and size/resilience-based Hit Points.
- Objects are immune to Poison and Psychic damage.
- The DM determines whether a damage type is especially effective or ineffective against a particular object.
- Objects have no Ability Scores unless a rule supplies them, cannot make ability checks and fail all saving throws.

## Armor Class by material

- Cloth, paper, rope: AC 11
- Crystal, glass, ice: AC 13
- Wood: AC 15
- Stone: AC 17
- Iron or steel: AC 19
- Mithral: AC 21
- Adamantine: AC 23

## Suggested Hit Points

| Size | Fragile | Resilient |
|---|---:|---:|
| Tiny | 2 | 5 |
| Small | 3 | 10 |
| Medium | 4 | 18 |
| Large | 5 | 27 |

Huge and Gargantuan objects are divided into Large-or-smaller sections. Large structures can also possess a Damage Threshold when appropriate.
