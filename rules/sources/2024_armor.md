# Armor — provided 2024 table

Status: vom Spieler am 2026-07-21 bereitgestellt; verbindliche Quelle für AC, requirements, Stealth, weight und cost.

## General

- Light Armor: 1 minute to don or doff.
- Medium Armor: 5 minutes to don, 1 minute to doff.
- Heavy Armor: 10 minutes to don, 5 minutes to doff.
- Shield: Utilize action to don or doff.
- An unmet listed Strength score reduces Speed by 10 feet.
- A listed Stealth disadvantage applies to Dexterity (Stealth) checks.
