## Level 3: Bonus Proficiencies

Gain proficiency with three Skills of choice.

Nathan selections:

- `Arcana`
- `History`
- `Insight`

## Level 3: Cutting Words

When a visible creature within 60 feet makes a damage roll or succeeds on an ability check or attack roll, Nathan may use his Reaction and expend one Bardic Inspiration. Roll the Bardic Inspiration die and subtract it from the creature’s roll, reducing damage or potentially changing a success into a failure.

At Bard 3, the die is d6 and shares Nathan’s three Bardic Inspiration uses per Long Rest.

## Later features recorded for progression

- Level 6, Magical Discoveries: two always-prepared eligible Spells selected from Cleric, Druid, or Wizard lists; replacements possible when gaining a Bard level.
- Level 14, Peerless Skill: after Nathan fails an ability check or attack roll, spend Bardic Inspiration to add the die; on continued failure the use is not expended.
