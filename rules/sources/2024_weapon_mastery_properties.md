# Weapon Mastery Properties — provided 2024 rules text

Status: vom Spieler am 2026-07-21 vollständig bereitgestellt; verbindliche Quelle.

- **Cleave:** after a melee hit, one additional attack against a second qualifying nearby creature; once per turn, without a positive ability modifier to the second damage roll.
- **Graze:** on a miss, deal damage equal to the attack ability modifier; only that modifier can increase it.
- **Nick:** make the Light property's extra attack as part of the Attack action rather than as a Bonus Action; once per turn.
- **Push:** on a hit, push a Large-or-smaller creature up to 10 feet straight away.
- **Sap:** on a hit, impose Disadvantage on the creature's next attack roll before the start of the attacker's next turn.
- **Slow:** on a damaging hit, reduce Speed by 10 feet until the start of the attacker's next turn; no stacking beyond 10 feet.
- **Topple:** on a hit, Constitution saving throw against DC 8 + attack ability modifier + Proficiency Bonus; failure causes Prone.
- **Vex:** on a damaging hit, gain Advantage on the next attack roll against that creature before the end of the attacker's next turn.

## Nathan implementation

- Rapier: Vex
- Hand Crossbow: Vex
- Dagger: Nick, but not currently selected for Weapon Mastery
- Nick would not combine with Nathan's Rapier because Rapier lacks Light
